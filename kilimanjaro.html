📅 Best Time to Climb Kilimanjaro - Month by Month

Weather determines your success rate. Pick your month:

Jan - Feb: WARMEST & DRIEST ☀️
Weather: Warmest months, 20-25°C base, -5°C summit. Clear mornings, afternoon clouds.
Success Rate: 90%+ on 7-day routes. Best acclimatization weather.
Crowds: Peak season. Book 4-5 months early.
Price: High season. $2,495 standard.
Best For: First-timers who want warmest conditions.
Mar - May: LONG RAINS - AVOID 🌧️
Weather: Heavy daily rains. Trails = mud rivers. Poor visibility.
Success Rate: Drops to 60%. High risk of slips, hypothermia.
Crowds: Almost empty. Many operators close.
Price: Cheapest but NOT worth it. We refund 100% if you book by mistake.
Best For: Nobody. We don't run climbs Apr-May for safety.
Jun - Oct: DRY SEASON, CLEAREST SKIES ⛰️
Weather: Coldest months but driest. -10°C to -20°C summit. Crystal clear views.
Success Rate: 95%+ on 8-day Lemosho. Best month = August.
Crowds: Busiest time. July-Aug = peak. Book 5-6 months early.
Price: High season. $2,695 for Lemosho.
Best For: Photographers, people who want clear summit views.
Nov - Dec: SHORT RAINS, GOOD DEALS 🌦️
Weather: Short afternoon showers. Mornings clear. Warming up.
Success Rate: 80%+ if you avoid heavy rain days.
Crowds: Medium. Christmas week busy + expensive.
Price: Shoulder season - save $200. New Year summit popular.
Best For: Budget climbers with good rain gear.
